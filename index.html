About Me

I'm a 24-year-old man who loves computers, video games, technology, and eSports. I'm often looking at future technologies and gadgets to add to my collection of things I don't really need. Recently in the last couple years I have gotten into watching a few different kinds of eSports or "Electronic Sports." I'm not a thrill seeker but when there is something that truly excites me, I don't think there is anything that will prevent me from satisfying that excitement.

Beyond gaming and technology I played some sports in elementary and middle school. When I was six years old to about 10 years old I played baseball on a local team! I didn't have a position since we were still basically learning the game to have fun.

Eventually I found myself less interested in sports and more interested in Music! I picked up the violin at 11-years-old and played all the way through my Senior year of High School! Through my time playing an insturment in a group setting, I learned a lot about confidence, cohesion, and cooperation. As tacky as that sounds, playing an instrument in front of 100 or more people takes a lot of confidence. In order to be heard, you have to play as loud as the rest of the orchestra; and trust me. The audience can hear your mistakes. Every player is a cog in the machine of the music and the conductor is merely the driver behind the wheel. If the musicians are ill-prepared and ignorant to the piece they're playing, the entire ensemble can fall apart from one musician's lack of practice.

I've never been outside of the country. I've always wanted to go somewhere where I can actually stand where a pivotal historical event took place. The more history I learn the more I want to go to those places to compare the photos and truly appreciate how far we, as a civilization, have come since those terrible events took place.
